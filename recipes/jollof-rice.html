Ghana Party Jollof Rice

Description

This one is the party killa. The showstopper. Whether it's your birthday party or wedding, or if you just want a rich rice delight. This is your go to.

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 small piece ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1/4 cup long grain rice
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup meat stock

Steps

  1. Chop tomatoes, onions and bell pepper. Blend together with ginger, garlic, and scoth bonnet pepper.
  2. Add sunflower to a pot on medium heat and add in the blended mixture.
  3. Simmer for 20 minutes, and add spices and seasoning.
  4. Pour in rice and meat stock.
  5. Cover the entire surface of the rice with aluminum foil and lid to trap steam. Cook for 20 minutes on low heat.
  6. Open lid, stir rice gently, and cover again to cook for an extra 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with a side of your choice.
