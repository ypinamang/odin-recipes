Gentle Japanese Curry

Description

This Japanese curry will leave you wanting more. It is a family favorite. It is just the right amount of spicy. Nothing you can't handle.

Ingredients

  • Curry roux cubes
  • Protein of your choice
  • Vegetables
  • Butter
  • Japanese Worcester sauce

Steps

  1. Add oil and butter in pan and cook onion until lightly caramelized.
  2. Add all vegetables and cook for about 2 minutes.
  3. Add water and bring to a boil. Turn down the heat and simmer for 8 minutes until the potatoes are tender.
  4. Dissolve roux and mix in the Worcester souce. Simmer for about 2 minutes
  5. Serve with rice and enjoy!
