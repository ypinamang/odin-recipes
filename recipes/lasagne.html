Delicious Homemade Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a family favorite. Everyone loves lasagne. I love lasagne, you love lasagne, we all love lasagne. The combination of hearty carbs, tomatoes and beef makes this dish such a delight.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • about 750g lean ground beef
  • 90g prosciutto
  • 400g of basic tomato sauce
  • 200ml beef stock
  • nutmeg
  • 300g of fresh lasagne sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g ball of mozzarella

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil and a frying pan and cook the lean beef for about 10 minutes.
  2. Chop 4 slices of prosciutto and stir in meat.
  3. Pour tomato sauce in 200ml beef stock. Throw in a little grated nutmeg to season.
  4. Bring up to a boil, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Heat oven to 180°C.
  6. Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets.
  7. Repeat until you have three layers of pasta.
  8. Sprinkle about 125g of torn mozzarella on top.
  9. Arrange the remaining prosciutto on top and bake for 45 minutes until lightly browned.
